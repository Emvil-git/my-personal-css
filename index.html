>>WHAT I DO

ART

"Always closer to Him are those cursed with the same penchant for Creation."

The curse of the creative is that, for its mind, creation is an act as involuntary, maybe even as necessary, as breathing.

When this act of creating is not directed, it will direct itself and create something.

Anything.

Most often, it creates misery.

FITNESS

"The Strong hath earned the right to be Evil. Thus, the Virtuous must be strong!"

Life is chaotic and unjust, as is nature. The virtuous strong, through their respect and loyalty to nature, are compelled to love it.

The greatest glory is that which is worth enduring well endured.

Steel yourselves in flesh for there is nothing but the body, in it are all our virtues and loves to cherish, and our faults to surmount.

PHILOSOPHY

"The Eternal Internal War, we call it - a most violent reaction to our mortality.".

The sadness of philosophers and even by those who "think about life too much" betrays just how horrible and miserable the nature of life is. If life is so wonderful, shouldn't thinking more about it, its nature, and its workings bring someone joy?

Behold, the Eternal Internal, the War without end!

Glory to whomever embarks on this deathless Hegelian conquest for they are the vanguards of our Humanity.
